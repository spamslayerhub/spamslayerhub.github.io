About Us

​ We are a group of reddit moderators that decided to come toghether to adress problems we all had with the plataform, notably, spam. By adding up our expertise in different fields we were able to create different tools and projects to make your life as a moderator easier.

​ All of the services that we pubicly provide are free and open source (even this very website), so feel free to experiemnt with your own versions and configurations, if you need anything feel free to join our development/help discord server!

Our Projects

  1. HSpamSlayer

    ​ A bot made to keep away undesired cosspost spam from malicious subreddits hosted by bad actors trying to promote scam links. To get this service you just need to follow these instructions. Also if you know python feel free to contribute!

  2. AutoMod Anti-Spam Cheat Sheet

    ​ A big compilation of essential anti-spam rules for Auto Mod that will help you filter unwanted links and messages from your comments and posts. A page dedicated to this is coming soon!

Project Tools

  1. HSpamSlayer Sub Config Helper

    ​ A tool to help you manage and create a HSpamSlayer configuration files for specific subreddits that you moderate, with helpful descriptions, great defaults and filters that help your config file be processed without any errors.
